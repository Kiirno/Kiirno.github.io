Thugs and Super Combatants.

As a top research nation with many good summons in conjuration, Sauromatia can expect to get a few of the elemental royalties.
In this section we will go over how to make a cost-efficient thug, a strong super combatant, and the commanders that will fulfill those roles.
However, for many of these, a pretender with paths will be needed.



Elemental Royalty

King of Elemental Fire.

As an F5 spell, King of Elemental Fire will be cast with an F3 Flame spirit with Skull of Fire and Flame Helmet, or your pretender.
The Kings of Fire have high HP as well as heat aura and fire shield, which makes them very good Super Combatants.
They also have darkvision.

Queen of Elemental Air.

An A5 Spell than can be cast natively with a Tome of High Power and a Fairy Queen.

Death Summons

Summon Shade Beasts

School: Conjuration 4 Casting: D2 Cast Cost: 15 Death Gems Summon: Shade Beast x 15+++

You can recruit a lot in a short time and they are ethereal.

Spirit Mastery

School: Conjuration 5 Casting: D1 Cast Cost: 4 Death Gems  Summon: Dispossessed Spirit x 15+

Spirit Mastery lets Sauromatia cheaply create 15 ethereal chaff using any of its death mages.
Getting conj5 relatively early is a good way to overwhelm someone who doesn't have magic weapons.

Legion of Wights

School: Conjuration 9 Casting: D6 Cast Cost: 30 Death Gems Summon: Wight x 20+++

Late game summon that gives you line-infantry worth a damn.

Tartarian Gate

School: Conjuration 9 Casting: D7 Cast Cost: 10 Death Gems Summon: Tartarian

10% of Tartarians are commanders and 50% of Tartarians are feebleminded.
The non-feebleminded Tartarians can be made commanders with either Gift of Reason(20 nGems) or Divine Name(25 sGems).
What you get for your trouble is a Super Combatant that has a good chance at incredibly strong crosspaths.
